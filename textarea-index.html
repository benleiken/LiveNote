timepad was designed by...

...a student, for students, to solve a very specific problem that sometimes plagues students. Any student, at at least one point in his or her academic career, may be expected to watch, analyze, and annotate a movie in explicit detail. A proper analysis and interpretation of any film must take into account sound, editing, cinematography, directing, and the thousands of other elements that go into a great movie.

click the button to start the timer, and type away!
